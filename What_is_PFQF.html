What is PFQF?

Public Fund Questioning Forum Nepal (PFQF Nepal) is a non-governmental organization committed to promoting transparency, accountability, and good governance in Nepal. Our mission is to empower citizens with the information and tools they need to question about the allocation and utilization of public funds. Our mission is to ensure a just and equitable society for all. PFQF Nepal has some specific objectives through which it wants to promote citizen's participation and surveillance over the public funds and its spending. It aims to conduct activities related to public funds, i.e., public income and expenditure; acquisition of funds; expenditure processes and their beneficiaries so as to institutionalize the effectiveness of public funds in terms of development projects. Furthermore, the PFQF Nepal will contribute to the policy formulation regarding the effectiveness of public fund through action research. It wil assist in localizing knowledge, skills, and best practices in collaboration with national and international organizations working in this field.
